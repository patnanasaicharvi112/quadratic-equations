This is Problem-1(iv) from Exercise 4.2
\myvec{{16}x^2-{8} x +{1} }= 0\\

\solution\\
\myvec{x=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}}\\
\myvec{x=\frac{8\pm\sqrt{8^2-4 \times 16\times1}}{2 \times 16}}\\
\myvec{x=\frac{8+\sqrt{64-64}}{32}}\\
\myvec{x=\frac{8+\sqrt{0}}{32}}\\
\myvec{x=\frac{8}{32}}\\
\myvec{x=\frac{1}{4}}\\
